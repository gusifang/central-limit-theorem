Central Limit Theorem Dice Lab

Bag and roll parameters

Imagine a bag full of dice. On each run, the bag is emptied onto a table, all D dice are rolled, and their sum is counted. Repeating that process R times adds R totals to the histogram.

Each run adds one total from D dice to the graph.

Dice parameters

The six probabilities must add up to 100%. This lets you test a bag of fair dice or bag of skewed dice.

Probability sum: 100%

Mean of one die: μ = 0

Standard deviation of one die: σ = 0
